Information

Rába is a community sound system, built for the purpose of having an immaculate sword in the hand of the ever so small underground.

Our outstanding sound quality is powered by a pair of JMOD 2.0 synergy horns, and 4 SKRAM subwoofers. (In the process of building)

Upgrades are planned, and we accept donations to be able to keep rental costs as low as possible.

All technical and rental costs go to the technical staff, and for the upgrading and up keeping of the system.

All dates and locations of parties and projects where you can listen to Rába will be sent out via email beforehand, so subscription is advised.

We are happy to collaborate on any project, and feel free to reach out.
